How to Use

Input Parameters

  • Atta Flow Rate: Flow rate of atta in kilograms per hour
  • Premix : Dilutor Ratio: Ratio between premix and dilutor (e.g., 1:4)
  • Standard Premix Addition: Standard addition rate in grams per metric ton
  • Hopper Capacity: Total capacity of the hopper in kilograms
  • Actual Output (Optional): Measured output for deviation calculation

Results

  • Premix Output: Calculated output in grams per minute
  • Premix Required: Required premix in kg per hour
  • Hopper Emptying Time: Time until hopper is empty
  • Iron Level: Calculated iron fortification level
  • Calibration Status: Indicates if calibration is needed
